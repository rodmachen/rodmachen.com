The Fitness Industry Is a Mixed Bag

I don’t have anything specific to say here other than trying to learn how to be healthy, strong, and fit is a real adventure. I can say that no one I’ve found, not my most trusted sources of great information are the be-all end-all. It’s truly finding the nuggets. Sometime’s it’s horrible people with retrograde ideas about society but they really understand the biomechanics of barbell lifting. Other times it’s competent science-based folk but their specialty is bodybuilding. Gotta be really careful how much to take from that arena.

Bottom line: There’s lots to learn and lots of places to learn it from. I figure if I hear the same thing over and over from people that seem sane, that’s a good start. From there, it’s a lot of figuring it out as we go.
