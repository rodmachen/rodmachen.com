Lots of people head to Taylor for world-class barbecue, but heading down Highway 79 a little farther leads to the town of Rockdale, home of Brett’s Backyard Bar-B-Que, about 60 miles from Downtown Austin. Started a couple of years ago by the eponymous Brett, this mostly outdoor joint quickly made headlines after landing a place on Texas Monthly‘s “Top 25 New Barbecue Joints” in 2019. Situated just north of Lexington and Snow’s BBQ, Brett’s aims to add to the abundance of great barbecue in Central Texas with classic smoked meats (brisket, ribs, pulled pork, sausage, and chicken) and delectable sides like creamed corn, mac & cheese, and Nutter Butter banana pudding. We caught up with purveyor Brett Boren during these pandemic times to see how things were going.
