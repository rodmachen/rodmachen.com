American Fiction deals with a quaint notion, the idea of “selling out.” In this age of Instagram, influencers, and attention seekers of all kinds, it’s a rarity that anyone wouldn’t want to sell out as fast as they can. Throw in squeamishness around issues of race in this country, and filmmaker Cord Jefferson (the Emmy-winning writer of HBO’s Watchmen) has created quite a narrative cocktail.
