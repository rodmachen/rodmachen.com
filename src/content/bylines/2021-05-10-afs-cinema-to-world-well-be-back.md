Last week, the Austin Film Society announced its intention to reopen the AFS Cinema this summer, an exceedingly welcome piece of news after 15 very hard months in the world of movie watching. As a part of this enterprise, they’ve launched a fundraising campaign called “The Next Picture Show” to help open the doors.
